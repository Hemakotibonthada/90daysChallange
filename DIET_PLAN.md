## Phase-wise Nutrition Adjustments

### Phase 1 (Day 1-30): Build the Habit
- Focus on eating consistently — 4-5 meals/day
- Hit **2,300 kcal** and **100g+ protein** daily
- Reduce sugar in tea/coffee gradually
- Drink **3 liters water** minimum
- No need to count calories obsessively — just follow the meal plans

### Phase 2 (Day 31-60): Optimize
- Start tracking protein more carefully — aim for **120g+**
- Increase carbs on heavy workout days by 1 extra roti/cup rice
- Reduce carbs slightly on rest days
- Try meal prepping on Sundays for the week

### Phase 3 (Day 61-90): Fine-Tune
- Target **130g protein** consistently
- Eat more on training days, slightly less on rest days
- Notice how your body responds — adjust portions accordingly
- Consider adding whey protein if struggling with protein targets
